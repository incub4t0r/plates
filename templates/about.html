About plates

What is plates?

    I built this mobile-first web application to help calculate the plates I needed load for workouts.

What can I do with plates?

    You can enter in the weight you want to use, then assuming you are using a 45lb bar, the page will load the plates you need to load per side.

What is plates built on?

    plates is built on html, css, and flask. Currently plates is running on pythonanywhere.com.

I can do this in my head.

    This web app is a small portion of a larger project I am working on to track your maxes and compete against your friends on a leaderboard, which may or may never be released. Stay tuned!

Your calculator doesn't work.

    I am tracking an issue with 2.5 increments on weights, and that is a side effect of the math.floor function I am using. I plan to fix this soon.
